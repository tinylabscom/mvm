# Your First Conversation

Your assistant is running. Let's break the ice.

---

## Open the Chat

Type this in Terminal and press Enter:

```
openclaw dashboard
```

A browser tab opens with a chat window. That's where you talk to your assistant.

---

## 5 Things to Try Right Now

Copy any of these and send them as your first message:

**1. Test its writing**
> Write a short email declining a meeting politely. Keep it under 3 sentences.

**2. Try research**
> What are 3 practical ways I could save time on [your biggest repetitive task]?

**3. Check if it knows you**
> What do you know about me so far?

(It should mention your name and work from the personality setup.)

**4. Teach it something**
> Remember: I prefer bullet points over long paragraphs.

Your assistant saves this to memory and uses it in future conversations.

**5. Give it real work**
> Summarize the key points from this: [paste any article URL or text]

---

## What to Expect

- **First responses might take 5–15 seconds.** That's normal — the AI is thinking.
- **Your assistant gets better over time.** It remembers your preferences and past conversations.
- **You can be direct.** "That's not what I meant" or "Try again, shorter" are perfectly fine.
- **It makes mistakes sometimes.** Just correct it. That's normal with AI.

---

## If Something Seems Off

**Assistant gives generic responses (doesn't know your name)**
Your personality files might not have loaded. Try:
```
openclaw gateway restart
```
Then refresh the dashboard and start a new conversation.

**"Command not found: openclaw"**
Close Terminal completely (Cmd+Q on Mac), open it fresh, and try again.

**Responses are very slow (30+ seconds)**
This can happen during peak usage times. It usually resolves on its own. If it persists, check your internet connection.
